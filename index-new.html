Championing Resilience, Innovation & Sustainable Growth

The Presidential MSME Awards aim to recognise enterprises that have demonstrated remarkable resilience and innovation while bringing together key role players in the ecosystem to support small enterprise development. The awards highlight MSMEs, cooperatives, and ecosystem enablers that contribute significantly to building sustainable businesses and creating value in the economy.

MSME Objectives

Recognising resilient and innovative enterprises

The awards promote entrepreneurship and the growth of enterprises from local to national level. By recognising and appreciating the contributions of entrepreneurs and ecosystem players, the programme advances the development of MSMEs and fosters wealth, prosperity, and economic growth.

  • Honouring resilient and innovative enterprises
  • Recognising sustainable value creators
  • Catalysing development initiatives
  • Celebrating economic excellence

Honouring Resilient and Innovative Enterprises

To recognise MSMEs and Cooperatives that have demonstrated remarkable resilience, innovation, and inclusive growth.

Recognising Sustainable Value Creators

To elevate enterprises that contribute to job creation, local economic development, and national transformation objectives.

Catalysing Development Initiatives

To highlight the role of MSMEs and Cooperatives in advancing sustainability, youth and women empowerment, and disability inclusion.

Celebrating Economic Excellence

To provide a platform that acknowledges excellence, while catalysing future support and development opportunities for MSMEs.
