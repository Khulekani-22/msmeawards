Presidential MSME Awards
Green Economy Category

Leading the Transition to a Sustainable Economy

The Green Economy MSME Award celebrates businesses at the forefront of South Africa's transition to a sustainable, low-carbon economy — developing the renewable energy solutions, circular economy models, and eco-friendly products that will define the country's green future.

Eligibility & Judging Criteria

  • Sustainable products, services, or business practices
  • Contribution to renewable energy or waste reduction
  • Circular economy and resource-efficient approaches
  • Measurable environmental protection or carbon reduction impact

Judging Focus

What We're Looking For

Renewable Energy

Developing and deploying clean energy solutions that reduce reliance on fossil fuels and expand energy access.

Circular Economy

Designing businesses around resource efficiency, waste reduction, and the reuse or recycling of materials.

Environmental Protection

Creating products and services that actively protect natural ecosystems and reduce environmental degradation.

Sustainable Solutions

Offering eco-friendly alternatives across industries that align economic growth with environmental stewardship.
